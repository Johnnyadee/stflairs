SAINT FLAIRS AWARDS LIMITED is the leading company in the Awards, Promotionals, and Specialty products business.
Founded in 1985, we have a longstanding tradition of quality service delivery.
Saint Flairs Awards provides multiple services; we deliver premium award products and work with our clients to ensure that the intrinsic values inherent in these awards are truly brought to life.
St Flairs has been working with industry leaders to bring out the best of their workforce by creating golden moments with award products to inspire, recognize and motivate. Our drive is excellence and celebration of purpose- these are the roads fronting the journey of fulfilment.

  • OUR MISSION: To inspire and facilitate success stories through the offering of unique and premium products and services.
  • OUR VISION: To Inspire humanity for the greater good, here and everywhere across the globe.

Our uniqueness is that we are not just a business, we are a human resources, public relations and institutional protocol resort devoted to arming our partners, those who do business with us with the noblest ways to lead, relate with people and build legacies.

Saint Flairs Awards Limited is one of the pioneers of corporate awards, and recognition business in Nigeria. As an extraordinary organization, Saint Flairs aims to inspire growth, progress and healthy competition needed to drive development by stimulating the human capacity to do good and to excel. For over 35 years we have strived to affect individuals, corporations, and government with our vision and mission in promoting the culture of excellence and golden moments through the rich collection of products and services we offer.
